## Engagement Tips

### Best Posting Times
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 9-11 AM or 1-3 PM EST
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

### Hashtag Strategy
- Use 5-8 relevant hashtags
- Mix popular (#AI, #TechInnovation) with niche (#EnterpriseAI, #Microservices)
- Include technology-specific tags (#TypeScript, #NestJS)

### Follow-up Engagement
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Ask follow-up questions to commenters
- Share additional technical insights in comments
- Thank people for shares and reactions

### Content Variations
- **Week 1**: Technical architecture focus
- **Week 2**: Business value and ROI
- **Week 3**: Lessons learned and challenges overcome
- **Week 4**: Future roadmap and innovations
